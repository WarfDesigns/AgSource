Accessibility Statement

Ag Source LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience and apply relevant accessibility standards.

Measures we take

  • Include accessibility as part of our mission and values.
  • Review new content for clarity and inclusive design practices.
  • Train staff to support accessible communication.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define three levels of conformance: A, AA, and AAA. Ag Source LLC aims to conform with WCAG 2.1 Level AA and is actively improving any gaps.

Assistive tools

We design our website to be compatible with screen readers, keyboard navigation, and browser zoom tools.

Feedback and support

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of Ag Source LLC. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers or need information in a different format.
